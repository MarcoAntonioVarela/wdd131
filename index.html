Mexico City, Mexico

Mexico City, Mexico Mexico City is one of the largest and most vibrant metropolitan areas in the world. Nestled in the Valley of Mexico at an altitude of over 2,200 meters, it is the capital of Mexico and a cultural hub. Known for its rich history, it was built on the ruins of Tenochtitlán, the ancient capital of the Aztec Empire. Mexico City is famous for its stunning architecture, such as the Metropolitan Cathedral and the Palace of Fine Arts, its traditional food like tacos and tamales, and its iconic landmarks, including Chapultepec Park and the Zócalo, one of the largest city squares in the world.
