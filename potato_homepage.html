Why I Created This Website

In 2023, I had the incredible opportunity to work as a paid Data Analyst intern at Category Partners, LLC, located in Idaho Falls, Idaho. This company specializes in category management, consumer research, and analytics for the perishables industry, including Idaho potatoes. During my internship, I learned extensively about:

  • The potato growing and distribution process
  • Different potato types and their uses
  • Quantities, destinations, and consumer trends

My experience with their comprehensive analytics inspired me to create this website, sharing my passion and knowledge about Idaho potatoes. I hope to showcase the industry's beauty and innovation while celebrating its global impact.
