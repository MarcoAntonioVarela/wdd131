Top Potato Companies in Idaho

Wada Farms

Location: Idaho Falls, Idaho

Description: Established in 1943, Wada Farms is a family-owned and operated business that has become a leader in the Idaho potato industry. They offer a full line of high-quality fresh potato products and have expanded to include fresh potato, onion, and sweet potato sales and marketing.

Unique Aspects: Grows over a billion potatoes annually, employs sustainable practices such as wise land-use, water management, and crop rotation.

Sun-Glo of Idaho, Inc.

Location: Sugar City, Idaho

Description: Located at the base of the Teton Mountain Range, Sun-Glo produces high-quality Idaho Russet Potatoes. They are one of the largest Idaho potato shippers, with a modern facility capable of processing over 4 million cwt each season.

Unique Aspects: Strategic location and large processing capacity make them leaders in consistent potato quality.

Pleasant Valley Potato

Location: Aberdeen, Idaho

Description: For generations, Pleasant Valley Potato has been perfecting Idaho potatoes. They are committed to growing, packing, and shipping the highest quality potatoes with exceptional service.

Unique Aspects: Controls the process from start to finish, ensuring the healthiest and highest quality potatoes through family farming excellence.

Sun Valley Potatoes

Location: Idaho

Description: Sun Valley Potatoes, Inc. is the first Idaho potato farmer cooperative, supplying 280 million pounds of Norkotah and Russet Burbank potatoes to the fresh market.

Unique Aspects: Farmer cooperative emphasizing collaboration among growers. Owns a fresh pack processing plant, ensuring high-quality produce and shared success.
